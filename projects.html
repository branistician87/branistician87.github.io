Chocolate Bar Ratings: ANOVA & Effect of Cocoa %

  • Analyzed the “Flavors of Cacao” dataset to understand how company location and cocoa percentage impact expert ratings.
  • Used factorial ANOVA with Type II sums of squares to handle unbalanced data and interpret main effects and interactions.
  • Performed model diagnostics (residual plots, normality checks, variance homogeneity) and applied transformations where needed.
  • Summarized results in publication-style tables and plots using ggplot2 and reported practical implications for chocolate makers.

  • Explored the impact of resample size (R) and sampling variability on CI width and coverage.
  • Documented workflow in a fully reproducible R Markdown report with annotated code and figures.

Modeling Whale Dive Durations with Gamma MLE

  • Fit a gamma distribution to whale dive/swim times using maximum likelihood estimation in R.
  • Used numerical root-finding to estimate shape parameters and linked rate parameters to sample means.
  • Bootstraped the MLEs to estimate their sampling distributions and construct confidence intervals.
  • Compared model fit to alternative distributions and evaluated assumptions via QQ-plots and goodness-of-fit checks.
2025 — R, Maximum Likelihood
